There are several codon tables now:
- python_codon_tables package: https://github.com/Edinburgh-Genome-Foundry/codon-usage-tables, which uses:
    - http://www.kazusa.or.jp/codon/ (derived from NCBI-GenBack Release 160.0 [2007])
- Codon Statistic Database: http://codonstatsdb.unr.edu/
    - From 2022, every species in RefSeq https://doi.org/10.1093/molbev/msac157       
    - Use this one, can download the whole database flat (6 GB). Then turn CODON and Fraction into python dict a la pct
